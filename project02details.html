PROJECT TWO: RHYS-READER

Visual Project Summary

Visual Project Summary

This is an interactive Audio-Visual Video-conferencing Interface that has been designed to help two different users - Rhys, an 8 month-old baby and his grandparents, Oma and Opa, who have never met in person. The goal of this project was to help develop a relationship between grandparents and grandchild and I have tried to accomplish this by implementing a virtual version of Rhys' favorite books, "Little Blue Truck". It includes all the pages from the book as well as a soundboard that comes complete with all the unique sounds made by different animals in the book. Additionally, it is also indicated what animal makes the noise to serve as a learning tool that Oma and Opa can use and a "Turn-the-page indicator" to allow Rhys to learn when to turn the page. The interface also consists of sounds that Rhys loves such as the Ukulele or Squeaky Toy to grab his attention.
